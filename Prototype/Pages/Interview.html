Before Interivew

Click to View
Do your homework.
Prepare a list of likely questions.
Identify what the organization wants and needs.
Interview yourself for the position.
Practice and plan.
Reflect on previous interviews.
Figure out how to articulate your goals.
Be positive.
Get comfortable.

During Interview

Click to View
Dress professionally.
First Impressions matter.
Speak calmly and clearly.
Make eyecontact and show that you are engaged in the conversation.
Be calm during technical questions.
Have questions ready to ask.

After Interview

Click to View
Write up a follow up E-mail.
Give the recruiter time to get back to you.
If the recruiter is more than a week late in responding, send them a follow up email.
Relax, and don't stress about the decision until they give one.
